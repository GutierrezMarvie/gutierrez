Sisig

Ingredients:

  • Pork face (ears, jowls, and snout), boiled and chopped
  • Onions, minced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Calamansi juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • Chili peppers, chopped

How to Cook:

1. Grill the chopped pork face until crispy.
2. Chop the grilled pork belly into small pieces or strips. Set aside.
3. In a bowl, combine calamansi juice, soy sauce, and vinegar. This will be your marinade.
4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until golden brown and fragrant
5. Add chopped onions and chili peppers. Cook until the onions become translucent.
6. Put the chopped grilled pork belly into the pan. Stir and cook for a few minutes until heated through.
7. Pour the calamansi juice, soy sauce, and vinegar mixture into the pan with the pork. Stir well to combine.
8. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preference.
9. Let the mixture cook until it starts to sizzle and the edges of the meat become crispy. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.
10. Transfer the sisig to a serving plate.

Morcon

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo ground pork
  • 1/2 kilo ground beef
  • 10 pcs duck egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup minced onions
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1 cup finely grated Queso de Bola or cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced fresh tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup fried garlic
  • 2 pcs Chorizo de Bilbaod
  • 100 grams annatto seeds atsuete, pour 1/8 cup hot water and strain
  • fat pig intestine membrane for wrapping
  • salt and pepper to taste

How to Cook:

1. In a bowl, mix together ground pork, ground beef, duck egg yolks, onions, raisins, grated cheese, tomatoes and garlic. Set aside.
2. Cut a portion of the pig's caul fat and place a handful of the morcon mixture on it.
3. Spread out the mixture and top with 2 to 3 slices of the chorizo.
4. Roll the morcon like a cylinder and do the same with the rest of the mixture.
5. Arrange the morcon on a deep plate with the annatto water and put in the steamer filled to 3/4 and steam for 4 to 5 hours on a very low fire.
6.Let cool. When cooled, remove the morcon from the steamer and slice then place on a platter.
7. Simmer the morcon drippings until it solidifies.
8. Pour oil and dripping over morcon slices. Garnish with fried garlic on top.
9. Serve immediately with steamed rice.

Bopis

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs pork lungs
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 1 Knorr Pork Cube
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 3 thumbs ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon annatto powder
  • 3 Thai chili pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fish sauce to taste

How to Cook:

1. In a large pot, boil 8 cups of water and then add the rest of the boiling ingredients. Put the pig’s lungs into the pot and continue boiling for 1 hours. Remove the lungs, let it cool down, and then dice into small pieces. Set aside.
2. Heat oil on a clean pot. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger.
3. Add the diced lungs once the onion softens. Cook for 3 minutes while stirring.
4. Add vinegar. Cook for 2 minutes.
5. Pour 2 ½ cups of water into the pot. Let it boil.
6.Add Knorr Pork cube and bay leaves. Stir. Cover the pot and adjust the heat between low to medium setting. Continue cooking until the liquid reduces to half.
7. Add the carrot, chili pepper, and annatto powder. Cook for 3 minutes.
8. Season with ground black pepper and fish sauce.
9. Transfer to a serving plate.

Menudo

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. pork
  • 1/4 lb. pig liver
  • 1 cup potatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 piece carrot, cubed
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 piece lemon
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 pieces hotdogs, sliced diagonally
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 to 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to Cook:

1. Combine pork, soysauce, and lemon in a bowl. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
2. Heat oil in a pan.
3. Saute garlic and onion.
4.Add the marinated pork. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Pour in tomato sauce and water and then add the bay leaves.Let boil and simmer for 30 minutes to an hour depending on the toughness of the pork. Note: Add water as necessary.
6.Add-in the liver and hot dogs.Cook for 5 minutes.
7. Put-in potatoes, carrots, sugar,salt, and pepper. Stir and cook for 8 to 12 minutes.
8. Transfer to a serving plate.

Sipo Egg

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. shrimp, head and shell removed and deveiened
  • 20 pieces quail eggs boiled and peeled
  • 11/4 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 piece carrot, cubed
  • 1 1/2 cups green peas
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 ounces ham, minced
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • 3/4 cup singkamas, cubed
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to Cook:

1. Melt the butter in a cooking pan.
2. Add the cooking oil.
3. Once the mixture becomes hot, saute the shrimp for a minute per side.
4. Saute the onion and garlic.
5. Put-in the carrot and green peas. Add the ham.Cook for 3 minutes.
6.Pour-in the water.Let it boil. Cook for 1 minute.
7. Add the quail eggs and shrimp.Stir.
8. Pour-in the cream of mushroom and table cream. Stir once more until the ingredients are well distributed.
9. Add water chestnuts, salt and pepper.
10. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve.
